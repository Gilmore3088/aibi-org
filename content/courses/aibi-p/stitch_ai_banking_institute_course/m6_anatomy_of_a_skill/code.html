Institutional Standards

The 5 Core Elements

These components form the DNA of any robust automated banking skill.

Element Description What Bad Looks Like What Good Looks Like
Role The expert persona the AI must adopt. "Help me write a report." "Act as a Senior Compliance Officer specializing in KYC."
Context The background and institutional setting. No background provided. "For an EMEA private equity desk under Basel III norms."
Task The specific, measurable action required. "Summarize this." "Extract 3 risk factors from the attached LOM."
Format The technical output structure. "Write a long email." "A 2-column CSV with Header: Risk | Mitigation."
Constraints The guardrails and "never-do" rules. None. "Do not use bullet points. Max 200 words. Neutral tone."
