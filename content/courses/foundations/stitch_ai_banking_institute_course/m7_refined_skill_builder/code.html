Write Your First Skill

The RTFC Framework

Precision in banking AI is not accidental; it is engineered. The RTFC Framework is our institutional standard for crafting executable skills.

R

Role

Define the specific persona. Are you a Senior Risk Analyst or a Customer Concierge? The role sets the tone and expertise level.

T

Task

What is the objective? Be explicit. "Analyze loan data for inconsistencies" is better than "Look at this loan."

F

Format

Determine the output structure. Markdown tables, JSON payloads, or professional executive summaries.

C

Constraint

Set the boundaries. "Do not exceed 300 words" or "Use only the provided CSV data."

"The difference between a generic prompt and a Skill is the layer of institutional logic embedded within these four pillars."
