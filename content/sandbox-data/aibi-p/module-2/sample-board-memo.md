# Valley Community Credit Union
## Board of Directors — Q3 2025 Performance Summary

**Prepared by:** Sarah Kessler, CFO
**Date:** October 14, 2025
**Classification:** Internal — Board Use Only

---

### Financial Highlights

| Metric | Q3 2025 | Q2 2025 | Q3 2024 | Trend |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|-------|
| Net Income | $1.82M | $1.67M | $1.54M | Up |
| Total Assets | $412.3M | $406.1M | $389.7M | Up |
| Net Interest Margin (NIM) | 3.41% | 3.38% | 3.52% | Stable |
| Efficiency Ratio | 67.2% | 68.5% | 69.1% | Improving |
| Loan-to-Share Ratio | 82.4% | 80.9% | 78.3% | Up |
| Return on Assets (ROA) | 0.88% | 0.82% | 0.79% | Up |

### Loan Portfolio

Total loans outstanding reached $339.8M, up 4.1% year-over-year. Growth was driven primarily by indirect auto lending ($14.2M in new originations) and commercial real estate ($8.7M). Consumer unsecured lending remained flat at $22.1M. The 60+ day delinquency rate rose modestly to 0.74% from 0.68% in Q2, concentrated in indirect auto. Charge-offs totaled $310K, within budget.

### Membership Growth

New member accounts: 847 (vs. 791 in Q2). Net membership growth of 1.3% for the quarter after accounting for 214 account closures. Digital account openings now represent 38% of new accounts, up from 29% a year ago.

### Key Initiatives

- **Core conversion (Symitar to Corelation):** Phase 2 testing underway; target go-live February 2026. On budget at $1.1M.
- **Branch optimization:** Elm Street branch consolidated into Main. Annualized savings estimated at $340K. No material member attrition observed.
- **Digital banking upgrade:** Mobile deposit limits raised to $10,000. Positive member feedback. Fraud losses within tolerance.

### Outlook

NIM compression remains a headwind as the rate environment shifts. Management is targeting a Q4 efficiency ratio below 66.5% through continued operational improvements. The NCUA examination is scheduled for January 2026; no material findings anticipated. The Board should expect a budget proposal for AI-related technology pilots at the November meeting.
