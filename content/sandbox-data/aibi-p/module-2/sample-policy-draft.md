# Valley Community Credit Union
## AI Acceptable Use Policy — DRAFT v0.1

**Status:** Draft for internal review
**Author:** IT Department
**Date:** October 2025

---

### 1. Purpose

This policy establishes guidelines for the acceptable use of artificial intelligence tools and services by Valley Community Credit Union employees.

### 2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and volunteers who use AI tools in the course of their work at the credit union.

### 3. Permitted Uses

Employees may use approved AI tools for the following purposes:
- Drafting internal communications and memos
- Summarizing meeting notes
- Generating first drafts of marketing copy
- Research and analysis of publicly available information
- Brainstorming and ideation

### 4. Prohibited Uses

Employees must not use AI tools to:
- Make final lending or underwriting decisions
- Generate member-facing communications without human review
- Create content that misrepresents AI-generated output as human-authored

### 5. Approval Process

Employees wishing to use a new AI tool should notify their department manager. The manager will determine whether the tool is appropriate for use.

### 6. Training

Employees using AI tools should familiarize themselves with the tool's capabilities and limitations before use.

### 7. Compliance

Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action. This policy will be reviewed annually.
