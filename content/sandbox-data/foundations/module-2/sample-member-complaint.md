# Member Complaint — NSF Fees

**To:** memberservices@valleyccu.org
**Date:** September 22, 2025
**Subject:** Unacceptable overdraft charges — need resolution ASAP

---

To whom it may concern,

I am writing because I was charged three separate NSF fees totaling $87.00 on my checking account (ending in 4417) between September 18 and September 20. I do not understand how this happened.

On September 17, I deposited a $1,200 payroll check at the Main Street branch. I was told the funds would be available the next business day. Based on that, I paid my auto insurance ($214.50) and internet bill ($79.99) online on September 18, and used my debit card for $43.12 in groceries that evening.

Apparently none of these cleared because my deposit was put on a two-day hold that nobody told me about. I was charged $29.00 three times for transactions that would have been covered if my deposit had been available when I was told it would be.

I have been a member of Valley Community Credit Union for 12 years. My direct deposit has gone to this account every two weeks since 2013. I have never had a problem like this before, and frankly, I expected better. I called the branch on September 21 and was told there was "nothing they could do" and to submit a written complaint, which I am doing now.

I am requesting a full refund of all three fees ($87.00). If this is not resolved, I will be closing my accounts and moving to First National.
