Every prompt worth saving has four parts.

A reading · about three minutes

The bankers who pull ahead this year are the ones who write a prompt once and use it every week — not the ones who keep typing variations into a fresh chat.

Every prompt worth saving has four parts: a Role for the AI, the Inputs you'll paste, the Constraints (what to avoid), and the Output you want back.

"The chat version says 'rewrite this nicer.' The tool version names the role, names the constraint, and names the output. One is a chat. The other is a tool."

Common questions

Asked & answered.

01 How long will this take me? +

About six hours of reading and exercises. Most finish in three to six weeks at one or two modules a week. You have ninety days from enrollment.

The Playground is sandboxed; nothing leaves the Institute. Module 03 is built specifically to help you stay inside any reasonable policy.

No code, no math. If you can write a memo, you can do this course.

A verifiable digital badge that proves you shipped reviewed work. We're working with state banking associations and the NCUA on continuing-ed credit recognition.
