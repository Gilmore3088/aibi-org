The institutions that score in the Ready to Scale band share three operating habits that Building Momentum institutions usually do not. None of them require additional vendor spend. All of them require organizational discipline.

Quarterly impact-ledger review at the board level

Not a vendor demo. A one-page summary of every active use case, its measured impact, and its sunset date. The board asks questions. The AI accountable executive answers them. The dynamic shifts.

A bench of two named successors per credentialed leader

No single point of failure. If the CIO leaves, AI capability does not leave with them. The bench is documented and visible during succession planning, not invented during a transition.

Active customer-facing AI with explicit explainability

Per Personetics 2025 (via Apiture), 84% of consumers would switch FIs for AI-driven financial insights. Explainability is what makes those features compliant under ECOA/Reg B and durable under examiner review.
