Three pilots that work on free-tier ChatGPT or Claude, with zero customer PII:

Summarize a credit memo

Paste a non-PII redacted memo. Ask for a 200-word executive summary plus three risk flags with section citations.

Redline a procedure

Paste an old procedure. Ask for changes that align it to current FFIEC guidance language. Note every change before sending it for compliance review.

Compare two vendor proposals

Paste both. Ask for a side-by-side decision matrix on cost, integration, and exit clauses. The output beats a printed spreadsheet most of the time.

The point is not the output. The point is what the banker says afterward: “where else could this save us 20 minutes?” That sentence is how adoption starts in community banking.
