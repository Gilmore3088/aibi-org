# Safe AI Use Checklist

Use this before putting work into any AI tool.

## Strip Sensitive Data

- Remove customer names, account numbers, balances, transaction details, Social Security numbers, addresses, and dates of birth.
- Replace real identifiers with placeholders such as `[CUSTOMER]`, `[ACCOUNT TYPE]`, or `[DATE]`.
- Do not paste confidential bank records into public tools.

## Ask Clearly

- State the role, task, context, format, and constraints.
- Ask for a draft, checklist, summary, or options rather than a final decision.
- Tell the AI what not to do, especially for customer-facing or regulated work.

## Fact-Check Outputs

- Verify numbers, dates, citations, policy claims, and procedural steps.
- Mark uncertain claims as `[VERIFY]`.
- Keep human judgment responsible for final use.

## Escalate Risky Decisions

- Escalate legal, compliance, credit, privacy, complaint, or customer-impacting decisions.
- Use approved internal tools and workflows for sensitive work.
- When uncertain, classify the use case as higher risk.
