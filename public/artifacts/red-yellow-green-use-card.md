# Red / Yellow / Green AI Use Card

## Green Uses

Usually safe when no sensitive data is included.

- Drafting generic internal messages.
- Brainstorming training ideas.
- Summarizing public documents.
- Creating reusable templates with placeholders.

## Yellow Uses

Use approved tools, remove sensitive data, and review before use.

- Customer-facing draft language.
- Internal policy summaries.
- Operational checklists.
- Financial or risk analysis drafts.

## Red Uses

Do not use public AI tools for these workflows.

- Customer PII, NPI, account records, balances, or transaction-level data.
- Credit decisions or adverse action reasoning.
- Legal, regulatory, or compliance determinations.
- Authentication, fraud, or security-sensitive records.

## Review Rule

If an AI output could affect a customer, examiner, policy, control, or financial decision, a qualified human must review it before use.
