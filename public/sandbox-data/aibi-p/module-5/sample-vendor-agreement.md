# AI Document Processing Services Agreement

**Agreement Date:** January 15, 2026
**Agreement Number:** NVS-2026-0842

**Between:**
First Community Bank ("Client"), a state-chartered commercial bank with principal offices at 200 Main Street, Riverside, IL 60546

**And:**
NovusAI Solutions, Inc. ("Provider"), a Delaware corporation with principal offices at 1400 Innovation Drive, Suite 300, Austin, TX 78701

---

## 1. Scope of Services

Provider shall deliver AI-powered document processing services ("Services") to Client, including but not limited to: optical character recognition, automated data extraction from loan documents, and classification of incoming correspondence. Provider's platform shall process documents uploaded by Client through Provider's secure portal or API integration.

## 2. Data Handling

2.1. Provider shall maintain "commercially reasonable" security measures to protect Client data during processing.

2.2. Client acknowledges that documents uploaded to the platform may be temporarily stored on Provider's cloud infrastructure for processing purposes. Provider shall delete processed documents within 90 calendar days of processing completion.

2.3. Provider may use aggregated, de-identified data derived from Client's documents to improve its machine learning models and service quality. Provider represents that such data cannot reasonably be re-identified.

2.4. Provider's subprocessors are listed at novusai-solutions.example.com/subprocessors and may be updated from time to time. Client will be notified of material changes via email.

## 3. Compliance

3.1. Provider shall comply with all applicable federal and state laws. Client is responsible for ensuring that its use of the Services complies with all banking regulations applicable to Client.

3.2. Provider does not warrant that the Services meet the requirements of any specific regulatory framework, including but not limited to GLBA, FCRA, or BSA/AML.

## 4. Liability

4.1. Provider's total aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the fees paid by Client during the twelve (12) months preceding the claim.

4.2. In no event shall Provider be liable for indirect, consequential, incidental, or punitive damages, including but not limited to loss of data, regulatory fines, or reputational harm.

4.3. Provider shall not be liable for errors in AI-generated outputs, including misclassification of documents or incorrect data extraction. Client is solely responsible for verifying all outputs before reliance.

## 5. Term and Termination

5.1. This Agreement shall commence on the Agreement Date and continue for an initial term of thirty-six (36) months, automatically renewing for successive twelve-month periods unless either party provides ninety (90) days written notice of non-renewal.

5.2. Upon termination, Provider shall return or destroy Client data within sixty (60) days. Provider shall certify destruction in writing upon Client's request.

5.3. Sections 2.3, 4, and 6 shall survive termination of this Agreement.

## 6. General Provisions

6.1. This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.

6.2. Provider may assign this Agreement in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of substantially all of its assets without Client's consent.

6.3. Any disputes arising under this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in Austin, Texas.
