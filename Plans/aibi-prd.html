01 · Product Overview

What we're building
and why it matters.

Product Vision
A marketing and conversion website + assessment tool that (1) educates community bank leaders on AI readiness, (2) generates qualified leads via a free assessment, and (3) converts those leads into paid certifications and consulting engagements. The site IS the sales funnel.
Primary Success Metric
Assessment completion rate → Executive Briefing conversion. Every feature prioritization decision flows from this. If a feature doesn't increase assessment completions or briefing bookings, it is not MVP.
Product Principles
1. The assessment is the product. The free assessment is not a lead form — it's a genuine value delivery that earns the email address. Every assessment result must give the user something they couldn't get elsewhere.

2. Every page has one job. One primary CTA per page. No page tries to do two things at once. If a user can't tell what they're supposed to do next in 3 seconds, it's a design/copy failure.

3. No friction before value. Assessment results are visible before email capture. Email is requested for the detailed report. Trust is earned before data is extracted.

4. Mobile is not an afterthought. Banking executives read email on phones. The assessment must be completable on mobile in 3 minutes without friction.

03 · Assessment Feature Spec — The Core Product

The assessment is where
the business starts.

This is the single most important feature in the product. It is the lead generation engine, the value delivery mechanism, and the first impression of AiBI. It must work perfectly before the site launches.

Critical Requirement
The score and basic results must be visible WITHOUT email capture. Email is requested only for the detailed personalized report. Gating the score itself will destroy conversion. The value proposition is "we tell you where you stand immediately" — if that's not true, the assessment loses its reason to exist.

Results Page Requirements

  • Animated score ring (0 → score, 1.5s ease animation) visible immediately on load
  • Tier label and one-paragraph plain-language description of what the score means
  • 8-bar breakdown showing score per dimension (4-bar max, filled = score)
  • Three specific "what to do next" recommendations based on the score tier
  • Email gate: "Get your personalized AI roadmap emailed to you" — appears below the basic score, above the detailed breakdown
  • Primary CTA after email capture: "Request Free 45-min Executive Briefing" (Calendly link)
  • Secondary CTA for scores 8–14: "Start with AI Foundations — $97"
  • Score is shareable: "Copy my score link" generates a shareable URL with score embedded

04 · Key User Flows

Five journeys that
drive revenue.

Flow 1 · Assessment → Executive Briefing (Primary Conversion)
Homepage → CTA click → /assessment → 8 questions → score reveal → email capture → full results → "Request Free Briefing" → Calendly → briefing booked → HubSpot contact created → automated follow-up sequence triggered
Flow 2 · Assessment → AI Foundations Purchase ($97)
Assessment score 8–14 → results page → "Start with AI Foundations — $97" CTA → /foundations → course overview → Stripe checkout → payment confirmed → LMS access provisioned → welcome email → AiBI-Foundations certificate on completion
Flow 3 · Direct Certifications Interest
/certifications → AiBI Foundations or AiBI-L selection → program details → individual purchase ($295 or $2,800) OR "Request Info" form for group/institutional pricing → HubSpot contact → follow-up sequence
Flow 4 · LinkedIn / BAI Brief → Assessment
LinkedIn post with assessment link → /assessment → complete → score → email capture → briefing booking. This is the social-to-conversion flow. Assessment URL must work flawlessly on mobile.
Flow 5 · Conference Follow-Up Email → Assessment
Post-conference email with assessment link → /assessment → score → email captured → automated sequence: Day 0 (score email) → Day 3 (use case email) → Day 7 (briefing offer) → Day 14 (final follow-up). HubSpot handles the sequence.

07 · MVP Launch Checklist

Minimum viable
before the conference email goes out.

The conference follow-up email cannot be sent until every item below is checked. This is the hard gate on launch.

  • aibankinginstitute.com and aibankinginstitute.org domains registered and DNS configured
  • Home page live and rendering correctly on desktop and mobile
  • Assessment tool live at /assessment — all 8 questions functional, scoring logic correct
  • Results page rendering with animated ring, score, tier label, and next steps
  • Email capture form on results page connected to ConvertKit
  • ConvertKit automated welcome email sequence active (Day 0, Day 3, Day 7)
  • Calendly "Executive Briefing" link live and tested on mobile
  • HubSpot integration: every email capture creates a contact with score properties
  • Plausible analytics installed and tracking assessment completion events
  • SSL certificate active (HTTPS everywhere)
  • 404 page exists and redirects gracefully
  • Site tested on iPhone (Safari), Android (Chrome), and desktop (Chrome, Safari, Firefox)
  • Assessment completed end-to-end in under 3 minutes on mobile
  • All sourced statistics on site have citations (FDIC, Gartner via Jack Henry, AIEOG) — no unsourced data claims live on launch day
  • The word "FFIEC-aware" does not appear anywhere on the site
