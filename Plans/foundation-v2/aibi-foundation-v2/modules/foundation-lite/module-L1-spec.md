## Why Foundation Lite

Foundation Lite is the **literacy floor** for every employee at the bank. Tellers, vault, custodial, seasonal staff, board members during orientation. ~90 minutes total. Mandatory bank-wide.

L1 is the same activity as Foundation Full Module 1 — first prompt, multi-model comparison, daily-use outcome. The difference is what comes after: Lite finishes in 4 modules; Full continues through 20.

Lite graduates can later upgrade to Full by completing the remaining modules (3, 5–20).
