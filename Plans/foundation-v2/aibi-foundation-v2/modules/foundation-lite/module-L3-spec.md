# Foundation Lite — Module L3: Safe AI Use — Your Five Never-Do's

**Track:** Foundation Lite · **Duration:** 20 min
**Activity types used:** 3 (sorting / classification), 4 (adaptive scenario)
**Daily-use outcomes:** Personal Data-Tier Routing Card; Five Never-Do's signed acknowledgment

---

## Why this module exists

Lite condenses Foundation Full Module 4. Same drag-and-drop sort, same five never-do's, same signed acknowledgment. The Lite version uses a smaller item bank (12 items instead of 20) and tighter scenarios — the *core* skill is identical.

This is the most operationally important module in the Lite track. Every employee at the bank should be able to sort confidently and recite the five never-do's.

---

## Activities

### Activity L3.1 — The data-tier sort (10 min · type 3)

Same activity as Foundation Full Module 4 Activity 4.1 with a 12-item bank instead of 20.

**Capture:** correctly-sorted final state → Data-Tier Routing Card.

### Activity L3.2 — Never-Do scenarios (6 min · type 4)

Same activity as Foundation Full Module 4 Activity 4.2 — five rapid-fire scenarios, one per never-do.

**Capture:** correct/incorrect picks logged on the Routing Card.

### Activity L3.3 — Sign your card (4 min · type 1)

Same activity as Foundation Full Module 4 Activity 4.3.

**Capture:** signed Data-Tier Routing Card → `module_L3_data_tier_routing_card.md`.

---

## Daily-use outcomes

1. A signed Data-Tier Routing Card with the five never-do's
2. Internalized sorting reflex

---

## Assessment criteria

- 11 of 12 sort items classified correctly (after feedback)
- Correct answer chosen on at least 4 of 5 never-do scenarios
- Card signed
