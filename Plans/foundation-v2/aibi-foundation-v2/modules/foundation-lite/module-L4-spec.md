# Foundation Lite — Module L4: Talking to Members + Recognizing AI Threats

**Track:** Foundation Lite · **Duration:** 25 min
**Activity types used:** 4 (adaptive scenario — member dialogue + voice phishing)
**Daily-use outcomes:** Member Conversation Quick-Cards (3); Voice Verification Habit

---

## Why this module exists

Lite finishes with the two highest-frequency real-world skills front-line staff need: knowing what to say when a member asks about AI, and recognizing when a "voice" on the phone may not be who it claims.

This consolidates Foundation Full Modules 5 and 6 into a single activity-driven Lite module. Three quick member dialogues + one voice-verification activity.

---

## Activities

### Activity L4.1 — Three member conversations (15 min · type 4)

**Setup (1 min):** "Three short member conversations. Pick how you'd respond. The platform will show you what happens next."

**The activity:**

Three branching scenarios (~4 min each), drawn from the most common member-AI questions:

**Scenario 1 — "Are you using AI on my account?"** (universal)
**Scenario 2 — "I don't want any AI making decisions about me."** (boundary-setting)
**Scenario 3 — "I think you used AI on the wrong account — your letter mentioned things that weren't about me."** (potential incident)

Same scenarios as Foundation Full Module 6, with tighter branching for the Lite track.

**Capture:** three Member Conversation Quick-Cards → `module_L4_member_conversation_quickcards.md`.

### Activity L4.2 — Voice verification activity (8 min · type 4)

**Setup (1 min):** "A voice on the phone asks you to do something financial. The platform will show you four scenarios. Pick the right verification step."

**The activity:**

Four scenarios:

1. "Your CEO" calls asking to expedite a wire — branches test callback to known number vs. acting on the call
2. "A regulator" calls asking for member account information — branches test escalation vs. compliance
3. "An IT vendor" asks for system access for emergency maintenance — branches test out-of-band confirmation
4. "A member" calls but their voice sounds slightly off — branches test polite verification questions

**Capture:** Voice Verification Habit card → 4 simple rules:
- Always callback through a known number, never the one that called
- Out-of-band verification for any financial instruction
- Multi-person approval for thresholds
- Escalate immediately if anything feels off — the cost of a delay is small; the cost of a misstep is large

→ `module_L4_voice_verification_habit.md`.
