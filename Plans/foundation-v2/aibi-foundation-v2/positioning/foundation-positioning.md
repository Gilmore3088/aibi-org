## What it is

**AiBI-Foundation v2** is an activity-driven, multi-model, AI-powered learning program for community bank employees. Built specifically for $500M-scale community banks. Four tracks for four audiences. The first credential in a three-tier ladder; also a complete, standalone deployment.

- **Activities, not videos.** 80%+ of every module is hands-on with AI, producing artifacts the learner uses the next day at work.
- **Multi-model platform.** Activities run across Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, and (where available) M365 Copilot — so learners build judgment about tool selection through real comparison, not vendor marketing.
- **Banking-specific throughout.** Member emails, loan committee prep, regulatory bulletins, vendor pitches, deepfake voice phishing — every example is community-bank reality.
- **Every module ends in a usable artifact, not a quiz.**

## The four tracks

| Track | Audience | Modules | Time | Outcome |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **AiBI-Foundation Lite** | Every employee — tellers, vault, custodial, board orientation, seasonal staff | 4 | ~90 min | Literacy + safe-use baseline |
| **AiBI-Foundation Full** | Anyone using AI for work — customer-facing, back-office, lending, compliance, finance, ops | 20 | ~9.5 hrs | A practitioner with a tested Personal Prompt Library |
| **AiBI Manager Track** | Anyone supervising AI users | 3 | ~90 min | Coaching capability + library review skill |
| **AiBI Board Briefing** | Directors, audit committee, board observers | 2 | ~60 min | Governance vocabulary + question playbook |

Lite is the bank-wide mandatory floor. Full is for staff who'll actually use AI for work. Manager Track is for supervisors. Board Briefing is for directors. All four can deploy together.

## What's new in v2

- **Activities replace video lectures.** Every module is hands-on with multiple AI models.
- **Cybersecurity & AI Threats module** — the highest-leverage addition. Prompt injection lab, deepfake recognition, voice verification protocol.
- **Talking About AI With Members** — scripted dialogues for the conversations members are starting to have with staff.
- **Spreadsheet Workflows** — Excel + AI patterns, a v1 gap closed.
- **Vendor Pitch Decoder** — real (anonymized) vendor decks, scorecard production.
- **Incident Response Drill** — tabletop simulation producing a real incident response checklist.
- **Examiner Q&A Practice** — simulated examiner conversations producing a Prep Card.
- **How AI Got Here** — a 20-minute briefing the learner can hand to their CEO.
- **Foundation Lite, Manager Track, Board Briefing** — three new tracks alongside Foundation Full.
- **10 role families** in role-based use cases (up from 5) — adds BSA/AML, Internal Audit, IT/MSP, HR, Marketing.

## Why community banks specifically

- **$500M-scale assumptions throughout.** 1–3 person IT (often with MSP), CEO as AI sponsor, compliance officer wearing multiple hats, M365 + Copilot Chat as realistic baseline.
- **Regulatory anchoring.** OCC Bulletin 2025-26 community-bank flexibility, Treasury FS AI RMF (Feb 2026), May 2025 CFPB withdrawals, NIST Cyber AI Profile (Dec 2025).
- **Right-sized governance.** Teaches the "ask before you build" muscle without requiring a dedicated MRM team.
- **No code, no API, no engineering background required.** Learners interact with guided activities; the platform handles the API plumbing.
