## Red flags I observed

- ☐ Vague substance — repeated buzzwords without specifics
- ☐ No clear answer on data residency
- ☐ No model risk documentation available
- ☐ No audit trail / log access for our use
- ☐ Pricing structure that bypasses TPRM review (e.g., "included in existing contract")
- ☐ Resistance to one or more decoder questions
- ☐ Pitch timeline pressure / "decide this quarter"
