## My escalation triggers (ranked)

**Tier 3 — escalate immediately:**

- Member NPI exposure to public AI tool
- Confirmed deepfake-cloned voice acted on (wire / system access)
- Confirmed prompt injection that affected an action
- AI-influenced credit/account decision without human review
- Vendor AI feature in production without TPRM approval

**Tier 2 — escalate within 24 hours:**

- Repeat tier-mismatch pattern after coaching
- Shadow AI tool discovered with member-data history
- AI vendor pitch with serious data residency concerns
- Member complaint potentially related to AI use

**Tier 1 — document and coach:**

- Single-incident tier-mismatch (no NPI exposure)
- Pattern of decay across team libraries
- Quality concerns on AI output before it left the bank

**Tier 0 — coaching only:**

- Skepticism / under-use
- Workflow inefficiency
- Personal preference issues
