## My four rules

Never use the number that called. Use the number on file.

### 2. Out-of-band confirmation for any financial instruction

Different channel. A text, email, in-person, internal Teams.

## Tells of a deepfake voice

- Slight monotone or unnatural rhythm
- Lack of audible breath or filler words
- Background that's "too clean"
- Subtle phonetic artifacts on certain consonants
- The caller resists callback
- Unusual urgency
