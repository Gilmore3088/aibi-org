**Acceptable primary sources include:**
- The regulator's own website (FDIC.gov, OCC.gov, CFPB.gov, FFIEC.gov)
- The bank's policy or procedure document
- Your bank's compliance officer or BSA officer
- Your bank's posted rate sheet (for rates)
- The vendor's own website or contract (for vendor claims)
- A primary news source for current events

**Not acceptable as a primary source:**
- Another AI output
- A summary of a summary
- The same AI tool re-asked
