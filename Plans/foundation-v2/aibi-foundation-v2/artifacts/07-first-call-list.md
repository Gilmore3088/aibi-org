## When something happens fast

If the situation involves member NPI exposure, a deepfake-cloned voice, or a confirmed prompt injection, escalate to **Compliance + IT** simultaneously.

If the situation involves a vendor AI claim or pitch, escalate to **TPRM + AI Program Owner**.

If the situation involves a credit, alert, or member-impact decision, escalate to the **owner of that domain** (Lending, BSA, etc.) plus **Compliance**.

When in doubt, escalate. The cost of an extra call is small. The cost of a missed escalation is large.
