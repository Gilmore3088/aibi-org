## When in doubt

If a member asks something not on these cards, two safe defaults:

1. **Be honest about what you know and don't know.**
2. **Say "let me get back to you with a full answer"** — and do.

Never guess. Never improvise on AI policy. Defer to the bank's compliance officer.
