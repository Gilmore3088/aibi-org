# Member Notification Template

**Module 18 artifact · AiBI-Foundation v2**

A starter template for notifying a member about an AI-related data-handling incident. Adapt to specifics with counsel and compliance review before use.

---

## Subject line

[ Important update about your information ]

---

## Body

Dear [Member name],

We are writing to inform you of a recent event at [Bank name] that may have affected your information.

**What happened:**

On [date], we discovered that [brief, factual description of the event in plain English — what AI tool was involved, what data was potentially exposed, and how it occurred].

**What information was involved:**

The information potentially involved: [list specific data types — name, account number, balance, transaction details, etc.]

**What we have done:**

- We took immediate action to contain the situation, including [specific actions]
- We have engaged with [vendor / IT / compliance / counsel] to understand and address the exposure
- We are reviewing our procedures and training to prevent recurrence
- We have notified [internal escalation — leadership, board, applicable regulators per requirements]

**What we are not doing:**

[If relevant] We are not, at this time, [explicit clarifications — e.g., aware of any misuse of the information / changing your account / asking for any action from you to verify].

**What you should do:**

[Tailored to the situation. May include:]

- Review your account activity at [link / phone] over the next 30 days
- Contact us at [phone] if you notice anything unusual
- [If credit-impact:] Consider placing a fraud alert with the credit bureaus
- [If no action needed:] No action is required from you at this time

**How to reach us:**

If you have questions about this notice or your account:
- Call us at [direct line] — staffed [hours]
- Email us at [secure email]
- Visit any branch — we'll talk in person

**We are sorry.**

We hold ourselves to a high standard with your information. We did not meet it in this case. We are taking this seriously, and we are committed to doing better.

Sincerely,

[Name]
[Title]
[Bank name]
